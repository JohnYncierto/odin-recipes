Burger

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  3. In a bowl, gently mix the ground beef with salt and pepper. Be careful not to overwork the meat.
  4. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties about 3/4 inch thick. Make a slight indentation in the center of each patty to prevent it from puffing up during cooking.
  5. Place the patties on the grill or skillet. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on one side, then flip and cook for another 3-4 minutes on the other side for medium doneness. Adjust the cooking time if you prefer your burgers more or less cooked.
  6. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  7. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or skillet for about 1 minute until lightly browned.
  8. Assemble the burgers by placing each patty on a bun and adding your favorite toppings and condiments.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade burgers!
